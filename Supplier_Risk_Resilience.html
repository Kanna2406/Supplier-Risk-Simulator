Understanding the Metrics

What is the Overall Risk Score?

The single most important number that tells you how risky a supplier is right now. Calculated live from the eight risk factors.

How it is calculated:

Simple average of all 8 sliders. Range: 0–100.

• 0–40 = Low risk
• 41–70 = Moderate risk
• 71–100 = High risk

What is the Resilience Index?

Shows how well the supplier can absorb and recover from the threat. Think of it as the supplier’s “shock absorber.”

How it is calculated:

Inverse relationship to the Overall Risk Score. Higher risk = lower resilience. Range: 0–100.
